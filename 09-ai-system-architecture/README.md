## Why This Matters

As a solution architect, system design is the native skill. This section applies that lens directly to AI/ML: how do you architect a RAG pipeline for millions of documents? How do you design a real-time recommendation system? How do you make build-vs-buy decisions for ML infrastructure? These are the conversations that happen at the senior level.
