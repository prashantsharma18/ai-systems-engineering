# 02 — Math for ML

The mathematical foundations that underpin every ML algorithm. The goal is not academic rigor for its own sake, but intuition-first understanding that translates directly into knowing why models behave the way they do.

## Why This Matters

You can use ML libraries without understanding the math, but you cannot debug models, design architectures, or understand research papers without it. This section builds the mental models that make everything else click.
